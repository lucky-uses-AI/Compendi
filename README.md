# Compendi: The AI Design Operating System

**Compendi** is a prompt-based "Operating System" that turns your AI coding assistant (Cursor, GitHub Copilot, etc.) into a Senior UI Architect.

It provides a strict, token-driven framework for generating pixel-perfect interfaces, ensuring your AI doesn't just "guess" at design, but follows a rigorous system of Law and Structure.

## ‚ö° The Protocols

Compendi comes with two built-in "programs" you can run by simply asking the AI:

### üìú Protocol Apostle (The Scribe)
*Use this to clone existing websites.*
> "Initiate Protocol Apostle."

The AI will guide you through a transcription process:
1.  **Witnessing:** You provide a URL/Screenshot.
2.  **Extraction:** The AI extracts exact colors and fonts.
3.  **Translation:** The AI maps them to system tokens.
4.  **Revelation:** The AI generates an HTML preview for you to verify.
5.  **Inscription:** The AI saves the new style permanently to your library.

### üîÆ Protocol Messiah (The Creator)
*Use this to generate new styles from scratch.*
> "Initiate Protocol Messiah."

The AI will act as a Creative Director:
1.  **Prophecy:** You provide a vibe (e.g., "Cyberpunk gardening app").
2.  **Covenant:** The AI proposes 3 art directions.
3.  **Genesis:** The AI generates a complete design system (JSON tokens).
4.  **Revelation:** The AI generates an HTML preview.
5.  **Canonization:** The AI saves the new style permanently.
